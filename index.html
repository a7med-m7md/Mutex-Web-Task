ABOUT MUTEX


MUTEX is the biggest technological annual event hosted by IEEE Zagazig SB.The event mainly focuses on introducing the latest technologies incomputer science fields to the audience and setting a clear path for themto follow.

VISION

Banishing the gap between the latest technology trends and the current community awareness.

MISSION

Establishing a link between the leading companies/organizations in the latest technologies or the ones that support the tech ecosystem and our communityâ€™s CS students, Engineers, Startups, and Techies through sets of talks, training, and workshops.

GOALS

Spread technological awareness among university students, startups, and investors of Al-Delta region.
Give the students the work experience in the latest technologies through workshops.
Connect Al-Delta tech ecosystem with resources and opportunities.



WHAT TO EXPECT AT MUTEX



LEARNING

Banishing the gap between the latest technology trends and the current community awareness.

PRACTISING

Practice in hands-on/interactive workshops to gain practical experience

NETWORKING

Meet Enterprise, Startups, Investors, and Techies who will all gather in one inspiring space for two days and connect with the Tech ecosystem.

PITCHING

Are you a Tech or Tech-enabled startup? This is an opportunity to fund your business and connect with investors, gain coverage in media publications, and extend to potential customers in Al-Delta region. And also an opportunity to apply in our pitching competitions.
